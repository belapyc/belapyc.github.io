Collaboration with **Dr Sinan Khadhouri** (NHS Fife) and the multi-centre **IDENTIFY Study Group** on early detection of urinary tract cancers in patients referred to secondary care with haematuria.

We developed **machine-learning models** trained on patient demographics, medical history, and diagnostic results to better predict bladder and upper-tract cancer risk, and performed **external validation** across an international cohort to test the calibration and generalisability of the IDENTIFY Risk Calculator in real-world clinical settings. Published in _European Urology Focus_ (2024).
